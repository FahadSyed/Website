Tempura
I created an Android based Time/Event tracking app that works like a stopwatch but allows you to add details for later viewing. The stopwatch runs in the background, so even if your screen is off or if you are using another Android app, your event is still being logged. All the data is stored onto your device, so you can close the app, restart your phone, do whatever, and your events will not be lost. Even if you un-install the app and reinstall.
